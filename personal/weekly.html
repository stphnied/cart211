Imagine you go back in time to the 1970s to change the history of the internet to your own liking. Based on the events described in the reading, how would you do it? How much funding would you need to achieve the changes you seek? Where do you get that funding from at the time, and how difficult is it to obtain? Honestly, I don't think I would be able to change the history of the internet even if I went back in time since the whole process seems a bit too complicated. If I had to change something, I would prefer to have less involvement with the military and give more power to the netizens. If netizens were granted access to the Internet and make it open-source earlier, I feel like they would've achieved a lot more than they did back then. However, it would be a lot harder to get fund from the government since the initial point was for the military. The great man theory shouldn’t not glorify the sole creator but acknowledge the rest...
